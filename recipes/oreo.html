Oreo Bon Bons

Description

These Oreo bon bons are a great dessert and easy to make. They can be great for birthdays and holidays. Combining crushed Oreos, cream cheese, and choclate these bon bons can satisy anyone's sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • (1 lb) package of Oreo cookies
  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • (16 ounce) package baking chocolate
  • (16 ounce) package melting chocolate

Steps

  1. Place Oreos in a food processor until roughly chopped.
  2. Add cream cheese and process until completely combined.
  3. Line baking pan with aluminum foil.
  4. Roll into ball and place onto foil.
  5. Place in freezer until firm.
  6. Melt chocolate according to directions on package.
  7. Dip balls into chocolate to cover, place back on foil.
  8. Melt chips according to package.
  9. Pour melted chips into ziplcok bag and cut the top off one corner.
  10. Drizzle as desired.
  11. Let chocolate set and serve.
